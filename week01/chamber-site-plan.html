Overview

Purpose

Pasadena Chamber of Commerce acts to provide a place for companies and individuals to build the community and encourage investment in the City of Pasadena through community outreach and tourism.

Scenarios

  • Why should I target Pasadena Texas as a good place to build or expand my business?
  • What events does the chamber of commerce hoast that will allow me to meat others doing business in Pasadena?
  • What are some of the demographics important to building a business in Pasadena?
  • How can I build my labor force in Pasadena?
